Who I am

Saira Mueller is the Tech Brew Editor at Morning Brew, a journalist, moderator, and presenter based in New York City.

Saira is currently the Tech Brew Editor at Morning Brew, where she covers the intersection of technology, culture, and wellness. Previously, she edited and wrote for WIRED (Gaming Editor, 2020-2022), CNN, Polygon (Interim Culture Editor, 2024), and Mashable.

She has moderated panels at some of the world's biggest conferences, including SXSW and the MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference, as well as presenting awards and hosting shows.

While she started her journalism career in print (newspapers and magazines) in 2012, Saira has become adept at online media and prefers to write and edit for the web and social media. She spends most of her time tracking her sleep, mood, habits, fitness, and health. When she's not doing that she's in a sensory deprivation tank, playing with her dog, and traveling the world.

Saira has a Masters in Journalism from Columbia University and holds Australian and German citizenship alongside her U.S. green card.
