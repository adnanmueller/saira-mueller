Speaking

Saira's conference debut was in 2016, when she moderated a SXSW Sports panel titled "Esports Goes Mainstream" to a packed-out room. Since then she has moderated and spoken about esports, sports, media, and technology at various conferences, including SXSW 2018, the MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference in 2018, and the Future of Television Conference in 2021.

SXSW 2016 - "Esports Goes Mainstream"

Saira moderated this landmark panel exploring how esports was breaking into mainstream entertainment. The packed room signaled the growing interest in competitive gaming as a spectator sport and media phenomenon.

MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference 2018

Speaking at one of the world's premier sports analytics conferences, Saira discussed the intersection of data, esports, and traditional sports media. View speaker profile

Future of Television Conference 2021

At this forward-looking conference, Saira shared insights on how streaming, gaming, and interactive media are reshaping the television landscape. View speaker profile
